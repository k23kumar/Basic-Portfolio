About Me

My name is Kelvin Kumar. I am 28 years old. I currenly work as a Production Assistant at the Lawrence Hall of Science at the University of California, Berkeley.

My favorite part of the job is creating exhibits and classroom materials. This requires creativity, skill and problem solving. I enjoy working with my hands and learning techniques and processes from the veteran members of our team.

I realize the value of my work when I see kids engaging in the exhibits and materials I helped create. It's great to see that my work directly fosters science education to our future scientists.
